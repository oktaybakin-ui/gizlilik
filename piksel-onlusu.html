Ozet

Veri Toplama

Uygulama; hesap istemez, analitik icermez, reklam icermez, ucuncu taraf SDK'si barindirmaz ve internet baglantisi gerektirmez. Hicbir kisisel bilgi (isim, e-posta, konum, cihaz tanimlayicisi vb.) toplanmaz.

Cihazda Saklanan Veriler

"Paylas" dugmesi yalnizca cihazinizin sistem paylasim menusunu acar; hangi uygulamayla ve kiminle paylasacaginiz tamamen sizin kontrolunuzdedir.

Privacy Policy (English)

Piksel Onlusu collects, stores and shares no personal data whatsoever. The app requires no account, contains no analytics, no ads and no third-party SDKs, and works fully offline. High scores, achievements and settings are stored only in your device's local storage and are deleted when you remove the app. The Share button merely opens your device's native share sheet.
